The concept:

A main article is produced to merely provide inspiration. From there users submit a response(a tweest). The tweest is the product of your imagination. It can be fiction or not. The sole purpose is to entertain and to get better at writing by composing a short story based on the inspiration. From there you get feedback from other users(hopefully helpful, witty,etc).

Be nice, have fun, improve your writing chops. Oh and the tweest must be between 250 and 10,000 words.

You can also form your own group(public or private) and get friends and family and other users to participate in it. Public groups should have a theme(sports, fashion, nature, etc) making it easier for their group to be classified and thus found. More details can be found when forming your group. But start with the main forum found on the Home Page.

It is hoped that this platform will useful in education situations.
